Appendix G: Commands Overview
#############################

+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Command**                                   | **Description**                                                                        |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `canceljob <commands/canceljob.html>`__       | cancel job                                                                             |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `changeparam <commands/changeparam.html>`__   | change in memory parameter setting                                                     |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `checkjob <commands/checkjob.html>`__         | provide detailed status report for specified job                                       |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `checknode <commands/checknode.html>`__       | provide detailed status report for specified node                                      |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `diagnose <commands/diagnose.html>`__         | provide diagnostic report for various aspects of resources, workload, and scheduling   |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `mjobctl <a.gmjobctl.html>`__                 | control and modify job                                                                 |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `mprof <commands/profiler.html>`__            | profile historical system performance                                                  |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `releasehold <commands/releasehold.html>`__   | release job defers and holds                                                           |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `releaseres <commands/releaseres.html>`__     | release reservations                                                                   |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `resetstats <commands/resetstats.html>`__     | reset scheduler statistics                                                             |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `runjob <commands/runjob.html>`__             | force a job to run immediately                                                         |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `schedctl <commands/schedctl.html>`__         | manage scheduler activity                                                              |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `sethold <commands/sethold.html>`__           | set job holds                                                                          |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `setqos <commands/setqos.html>`__             | modify job QOS settings                                                                |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `setres <commands/setres.html>`__             | set an admin/user reservation                                                          |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `setspri <commands/setspri.html>`__           | adjust system priority of jobs                                                         |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `showbf <commands/showbf.html>`__             | show backfill window - show resources available for immediate use                      |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `showconfig <commands/showconfig.html>`__     | show current scheduler configuration                                                   |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `showgrid <commands/showgrid.html>`__         | show various tables of scheduling/system performance                                   |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `showq <commands/showq.html>`__               | show queued jobs                                                                       |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `showres <commands/showres.html>`__           | show existing reservations                                                             |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `showstart <commands/showstart.html>`__       | show estimates of when job can/will start                                              |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `showstate <commands/showstate.html>`__       | show current state of resources                                                        |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| `showstats <commands/showstats.html>`__       | show usage statistics                                                                  |
+-----------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
